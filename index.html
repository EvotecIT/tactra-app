Tactra Remote

Tactra is a focused Apple-native media remote for Home Assistant. It controls speakers, TVs, streamers, soundbars, receivers, and media activities across iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac.

Local-first by design

Tactra does not require a Tactra account or Tactra-hosted cloud service. Home Assistant remains the source of truth, and credentials are stored locally in the Apple Keychain.
